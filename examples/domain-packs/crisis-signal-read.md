# Example — Crisis Signal Read

## Situation

A monitoring team sees a sudden burst of mentions around a public incident and needs an immediate read before deciding whether escalation is necessary.

## Skill combination

- `workflow`
- `crisis-analyst`
- `triad-check`

## Why this combination

- `workflow` creates the operational frame
- `crisis-analyst` separates signal from noise and identifies the likely stage
- `triad-check` becomes useful if the next move affects product, messaging, and operations together

## Minimal flow

### 1. Use `workflow`

State:
- immediate question
- what is being assessed now
- minimum proof for the read

Example:
- question: is this noise, early escalation, or active crisis momentum?
- proof: enough evidence to classify stage and recommend a next watchpoint

### 2. Use `crisis-analyst`

Read for:
- dominant narrative
- spread velocity
- key actors
- uncertainty level
- next watchpoints

### 3. Use `triad-check` if needed

Escalate to triad-style review when:
- product behavior may need to change
- public communication needs coordinated wording
- operational response spans more than one function

## Why it works

The read stays disciplined without pretending that a crisis decision should be automated by the skill alone.
