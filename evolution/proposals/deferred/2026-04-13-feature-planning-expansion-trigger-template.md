# Proposal

## Proposed change
Extend the `feature-brief.md` sidecar with a small field that records why expansion is deferred and what real trigger should justify the next slice.

## Why now
The Crisis Monitor case made this gap visible, but not yet universal.

## Why the target surface is appropriate
The issue is in output legibility, not in `Core Moves` or skill identity. A template refinement is the narrowest safe surface.

## Why this is not just local residue
The pattern may generalize to other bounded pilots, but it needs more evidence before canonical adoption.

## Decision notes
Deferred until another real observation confirms that the gap is not specific to one product lane.
