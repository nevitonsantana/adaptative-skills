# Review

## What was actually proved
The first Efficiency Layer trio can be mapped onto a real product lane in a way that stays bounded, legible, and separate from AletheIA macro work.

## What remains local
The reference case should not turn Crisis Monitor operating rules into generic library truth. The lane is a field anchor, not a source of automatic canon writeback.

## Reusable pattern or learning
The trio is strongest when treated as one lightweight operating loop: shrink the slice, pause before accidental expansion, and close with a resumable note.

## Expansion decision
Do not add a fourth efficiency skill yet. The next useful evidence should come from direct pilot usage, not further catalog growth.
